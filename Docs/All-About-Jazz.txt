'''''All About Jazz''''' is an [[online magazine]]<ref>{{cite book|last1=Colwell|first1=Richard|last2=Hewitt|first2=Michael|title=Teaching of Instrumental Music|chapter=Web Sites|chapter-url=https://books.google.com/books?id=WrhmCgAAQBAJ&pg=PT1045|publisher=[[Routledge]]|year=2015|accessdate=October 31, 2015|edition=4th|isbn=1317350839}}</ref> and website for [[jazz]] enthusiasts and industry professionals. It is based in [[Philadelphia, PA|Philadelphia]].

==Overview==
Founded by Michael Ricci in 1995, ''All About Jazz'' is maintained by a volunteer staff of writers, editors, and musicians, and provides coverage of all genres of jazz from independent to major [[record label]] releases. As an [[advocacy]] site, All About Jazz aims to raise the awareness of jazz music, its history and its players. It has been awarded "Jazz Website of the Year" thirteen times by the [[Jazz Journalists Association]].<ref name="aaj-jja-award">{{cite web|url=http://www.jjajazzawards.org/|title=Jazz Journalists Association: JJA Jazz Awards - Past JJA Award Winners|publisher=jjajazzawards.org|work='''Manual search required''' (left column)|accessdate=July 23, 2014|archiveurl=http://web.archive.org/web/20140706100133/http://www.jjajazzawards.org/|archivedate=July 6, 2014|deadurl=no <!-- alternative link (manual search required), http://www.jazzhouse.org/  -->}}</ref>

Ricci launched [http://www.jazznearyou.com Jazz Near You], a jazz event discovery and listing platform in 2012.
