The following is an overview of events in '''2007 in film''', including the highest-grossing films, award ceremonies and festivals, a list of films released and notable deaths. The highest grossing film of the year was ''[[Pirates of the Caribbean: At World's End]]'', which was just ahead of ''[[Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (film)|Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix]]''.

''[[Pirates of the Caribbean: At World's End]]'' and ''[[Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix (film)|Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix]]'' have grossed more than $900 million, making them [[List of highest-grossing films|the twenty-second and twenty-fifth highest grossing films]] worldwide in history.
