'''''India Today''''' is an [[India]]n [[English-language]] fortnightly news [[magazine]]<ref>{{cite book|author=Douglas Bullis|title=Selling to India's Consumer Market|url=http://books.google.com/books?id=J4dWLuerJl4C&pg=PA46|accessdate=7 May 2016|year=1997|publisher=Greenwood Publishing Group|isbn=978-1-56720-105-5|page=46}}</ref> and news television channel. It was established in 1975 by Vidya Vilas Purie (owner of Thompson Press), with his daughter [[Madhu Trehan]] as its editor and his son [[Aroon Purie]] as its publisher.<ref name="HT">Bhandare, Namita. [http://www.hindustantimes.com/Brunch/Brunch-Stories/70-s-The-decade-of-innocence/Article1-700294.aspx "70's: The decade of innocence"] In. ''[[Hindustan Times]]''. Retrieved 29 July 2012.</ref><ref>[http://www.dnaindia.com/india/slideshow_indias-top-50-influentials_1218058-32#top "India's Top 50 Influentials"]. ''[[Daily News and Analysis]]''. Retrieved 29 July 2012.</ref> At present, ''India Today'' is also published in [[Hindi]], [[Tamil language|Tamil]], [[Malayalam]] and [[Telugu language|Telugu]]. The India Today news channel was launched on 22 May 2015.
