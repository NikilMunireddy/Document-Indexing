'''Hypsogastropoda''' is a [[clade]] containing  [[marine (ocean)|marine]] [[gastropod]]s within the [[clade]] [[Caenogastropoda]].<ref name="Bouchet">Bouchet P. & Rocroi J.-P. (Ed.); Frýda J., Hausdorf B., Ponder W., Valdes A. & Warén A. 2005. ''Classification and nomenclator of gastropod families''. Malacologia: International Journal of Malacology, 47(1-2). ConchBooks: Hackenheim, Germany. ISBN 3-925919-72-4. {{ISSN|0076-2997}}. 397 pp. http://www.vliz.be/Vmdcdata/imis2/ref.php?refid=78278</ref>

This clade contains two clades and one [[informal group]]:
* Clade [[Littorinimorpha]]
* Informal group [[Ptenoglossa]]
*  Clade [[Neogastropoda]]
