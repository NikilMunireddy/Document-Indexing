*The Year of the [[military veteran|Veteran]] in Canada
*The Year of Discovery{{Citation needed|date=February 2009}}
*International Year for Sport and Physical Education
*[[International Year of Microcredit]]

The year 2005 was the end of the International Decade of the World's Indigenous People (1995–2005).

* [[December 6]] &ndash; An [[Iran]]ian [[C-130 Hercules]] airplane [[2005 Iranian Air Force C-130 crash|crashes into a ten-storey building]] in a civilian area of [[Tehran]], the capital of Iran, killing all 94 people aboard and 34 residents of the building (128 total).
* [[December 7]] &ndash; The [[European Union]] [[top-level domain|TLD]] ''.eu'' is launched, and replaces ''.eu.int''. Initially this will be only for business purposes. From [[7 April]] [[2006]] onwards, EU citizens can also register .eu domains.

* [[December 12]] &ndash; Scientists announce that they have created [[mouse|mice]] with small amounts of human [[brain cells]] in an effort to make realistic models of [[neurological disorders]].
* [[December 14]] &ndash; [[Shakidor Dam]] fails in Pakistan due to heavy rain.
* [[December 16]] &ndash; The 43rd [[Mersenne prime]] is found.

* [[December 20]] &ndash; [[Aleksandër Moisiu University]] is founded in [[Durrës]], [[Albania]].
* [[December 23]] &ndash; [[Chad]] declares [[Chadian-Sudanese conflict|war]] on [[Sudan]], following a [[December 18]] [[Battle of Adré|attack on Adre]], which left about 100 people dead.
* [[December 31]] &ndash; Another second is added, 23:59:60, called a [[leap second]], to end the year 2005. The last time this occurred was on [[June 30]], [[1998]].
