{{nihongo|'''Avex Trax'''|エイベックス トラックス|Eibekkusu Torakkusu}} (stylized as '''avex trax''') is a [[record label]] owned by Japanese entertainment conglomerate [[Avex Group]]. The label was launched in September 1990, and was the first label by the Group.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.avex.co.jp/e_site/group/history.html|title=Avex Group {{!}} About Avex Group {{!}} History|work=[[Avex Group]]|accessdate=May 22, 2010}}</ref>
